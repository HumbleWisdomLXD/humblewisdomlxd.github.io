Resisting the Mark of the Beast Series

The Art of Active Non-Resistance

Six signs, six motivators, and six ways to guard your gates against harmful mechanisms. Plus several tools to help you mindfully resist the beast.

Introduction to Purposeful Leadership

Explore the foundational principles of purposeful leadership and discover how to shift from authority-based management to values-driven influence. Learn to identify your core leadership values and understand the difference between leadership as position versus leadership as responsibility to serve others.

Building Authentic Influence

Dive deep into the concept of behavioral integrity and learn how consistent demonstration of values creates the foundation for genuine influence. Discover practical strategies for aligning your actions with your stated principles, and understand how vulnerability and transparency actually strengthen credibility.

Decision-Making Frameworks

Master the art of making difficult decisions through principled frameworks that consider long-term impact over short-term gains. Learn the five-step Purposeful Decision Framework: Clarify, Consider, Consult, Choose, and Communicate. Practice applying these frameworks to complex scenarios.

Creating Systems for Positive Change

Understand how individual leadership must be supported by organizational systems to create lasting impact. Learn to design processes, cultivate culture, establish meaningful measurement frameworks, and build feedback loops that naturally encourage positive behavior.

Developing Others Through Leadership

Explore the multiplicative impact of focusing on the growth and development of others. Learn practical approaches for expanding leadership capacity in your team, creating mentoring relationships, and building sustainable change that continues even in your absence.

Sustaining Purpose in Challenging Times

Develop the inner resilience and external support systems necessary to maintain purposeful leadership during difficult periods. Learn practices for staying connected to your core purpose when facing pressure to compromise values.

The Ripple Effect of Leadership

Understanding how purposeful leadership creates expanding circles of positive influence across professional and personal contexts. This final part will explore integration of leadership principles across all areas of life. Coming Soon

Advanced Leadership Challenges

Practical case studies and advanced scenarios for experienced leaders looking to deepen their purposeful leadership practice. Coming Soon
