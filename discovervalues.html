Our Core Values Revealed

Here are our official core values with their definitions and examples. How did your discoveries compare?

INTEGRITY

Definition: We act with honesty and take responsibility for our actions.

Examples: Admitting mistakes, giving honest feedback, taking responsibility

TEAMWORK

Definition: We support each other and work together toward common goals.

Examples: Helping colleagues, sharing resources, giving credit to others

EXCELLENCE

Definition: We strive for the highest quality in everything we do.

Examples: Thorough research, perfecting presentations, going above and beyond

INNOVATION

Definition: We embrace new ideas and creative solutions.

Examples: Trying new processes, supporting bold ideas, challenging status quo

INCLUSION

Definition: We value diverse perspectives and ensure everyone has a voice.

Examples: Seeking multiple viewpoints, celebrating different approaches

AGILITY

Definition: We adapt quickly and respond effectively to change.

Examples: Pivoting strategies, embracing new market conditions
